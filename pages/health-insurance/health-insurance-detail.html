Health Insurance

Our Health Insurance plans are designed to provide you and your family with access to the best medical care without the financial burden. We offer a range of plans that cover hospitalization, outpatient treatments, and critical illnesses.

Key Benefits

  • Wide network of hospitals and clinics.
  • Cashless claim facility.
  • Coverage for pre and post-hospitalization expenses.
  • Maternity and newborn baby cover.
  • Annual health check-ups.

Coverage Details

Choose from our individual, family floater, or senior citizen plans. Our policies cover a wide array of medical expenses, including room rent, doctor fees, surgery costs, and more. You can also enhance your cover with critical illness and personal accident riders.
